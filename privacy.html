Privacy Policy

Last updated:

DevQuantum Studio is committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle data. This policy outlines what information is — and isn’t — collected when you visit our website.

1) Quick Summary

  • No personal data collected.
  • No accounts or user sign-ups.
  • No cookies or tracking scripts.
  • No analytics or advertising tools.
  • No data sharing or selling of any kind.

2) Hosting and Basic Logs

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages, a static site hosting service provided by GitHub (a subsidiary of Microsoft). GitHub may collect limited technical information (such as IP address and browser type) as part of normal server operations. This data is handled by GitHub according to their own Privacy Statement. We do not access, use, or store this information ourselves.

3) Third-Party Services

We do not use advertising platforms, analytics providers, or marketing tools that track or collect personal data.

However, our website does make use of the following third-party services to load fonts and icons:

  • Google Fonts – Fonts are served via Google’s CDN (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). Google may collect limited technical information such as IP address and browser details to deliver these assets. See Google’s Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Font Awesome – Icons are loaded via the Cloudflare CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). Cloudflare may log standard request data (e.g., IP address, user agent) as part of their delivery infrastructure. See Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy.

We do not access, store, or use any of this information ourselves.

4) External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Ko-fi, or social media). These sites are not operated by us, and we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies if you choose to visit them.

5) Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from minors.

6) Security

While we do not collect user data, we take general website security seriously. Our site is served over HTTPS, and all content is static with no backend processing or database integration.

7) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our website or practices evolve. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

8) Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us here.
